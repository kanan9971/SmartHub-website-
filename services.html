BVI Service

Explore Overseas Opportunities & Set Up Your Offshore Company!

The Whole Procedure

1

Company Name Check

We check whether your desired company name is available for offshore company formation and assist you in arranging the provision of required company director and shareholder details.

2

Document Submission

Our team prepares a complete set of offshore KYC and company registration documents for you to sign. If you are located overseas, you can send us digital copies of the signed documents.

3

Register Office

After you submit the completed application form and all relevant documents, the Company Registry will register your company offshore and issue you a Business Registration Certificate.

4

Supporting Tools

When we handle your company registration, we'll provide 10 stock certificates, a signature stamp, a common seal, and a company toolbox as complimentary accessories.
