How can we collaborate with you?
We are looking for investment, technological, accounting and educational services to increase our customers' experience. We also allow businesses to list their properties on our website.
Do I need to be based in Hong Kong to partner with you?
No, we collaborate with companies all over the world. Our network spans multiple regions, and we welcome international partners who share our commitment to excellence.
How do I apply to become a partner?
Use the partnership form above to contact us. Our team will review your submission and reach out within 3-5 business days to discuss next steps.
What is the cost of setting up an office in HK?
Our set up cost can be as low as USD$1,000, which includes business registration certification, professional fees, and government incorporation fees. Exact costs depend on the company structure and services required.
What other benefits does your company provide?
We can help with M&A listing. We also have part-time staff available for short-term needs such as marketing, so your partnership can scale flexibly as your needs change.
What is the fee for renting properties on your website?
10% of each transaction. This covers the listing, promotion within our network, and transaction support.
