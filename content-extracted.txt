Tab 3: BVI Service
Explore Oversea Opportunities &amp; Set Up Your Offshore Company!
Whole Procedure
1. Company Name Check
We will check whether your desired company name is available for offshore company formation and assist you in arranging the provision of the required company director and shareholder details.
2. Document Submission
Our team will prepare a complete set of offshore KYC and company registration documents for you to sign. If you are located overseas, you can send us digital copies of the signed documents.
3. Register Office
After you submit the completed application form and all relevant documents, the Company Registry will register your company offshore and issue you a Business Registration Certificate.
4. Supporting Tools
When we handle your company registration, we’ll provide 10 stock certificates, a signature stamp, a common seal, and a company toolbox as complimentary accessories.
